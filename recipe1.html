Pasta

This is a simple pasta recipe. It’s easy to make and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil, chopped

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the canned tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss the pasta with the sauce and garnish with fresh basil.
